### Key insights for your paper

1. **Foroughifar & Mehta (2024)** is your closest competitor — directly studies Airbnb Smart Pricing deployment effects. Must read and differentiate. Their focus appears to be on deployment *challenges* rather than market conduct implications.

2. **Huang (2021, rev. 2025)** documents that Airbnb hosts exhibit significant pricing frictions (prices unresponsive to demand), and platform pricing assistance increases surplus for all parties. This supports your "cognitive constraint" channel — the algorithm helps hosts who couldn't price optimally.

3. **Ye et al. (2018, KDD)** is Airbnb's own paper describing Smart Pricing. Essential for institutional detail. Confirms the system uses a binary classification model (booking probability) + customized regression model.

4. **Casamatta et al. (2021)** provides evidence that professional hosts perceive more market power during peak seasons — aligns with your heterogeneity findings about host sophistication.

5. **Piao (2023)** uses RDD on Airbnb — your most direct methodological competitor. Very low citations suggest either it's unpublished or has identification concerns. Must read to understand what they found and how your design improves on it.
