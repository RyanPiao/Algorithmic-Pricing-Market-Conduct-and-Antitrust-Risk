# Style Guide

## Voice
- Semi-formal academic tone
- Third-person throughout
- Active voice preferred over passive
- Lead with the result, not the method

## Structure
- Head formula for introduction (hook → question → method → finding → contribution → roadmap)
- Empirical sections: specification → assumptions → estimation → results → interpretation
- Tables before discussion; figures near first reference

## Citations
- Author-date inline: Calvano et al. (2020), Assad et al. (2024)
- Parenthetical: (Calvano et al. 2020)

## Numbers
- Report coefficients to 4 decimal places in tables
- Standard errors in parentheses below coefficients
- Stars: * p<0.10, ** p<0.05, *** p<0.01
- Sample sizes with commas: 24,228,568

## Tables
- Booktabs style (no vertical rules)
- Export both .tex and .md
- Always include N, R², clustering note
